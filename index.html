Services

Here you get the Impressive services.

Shipping

An automated platform where you can depend on Delhivery for orders placed on every sales channel, with lower fulfillment costs. We conduct quality checks at every return order pickup. We also prevent discrepancies by measuring weight at every touchpoint.

E-Commerce

We help in situations when a current ecommerce solution hinders. We unfold an ecommerce process to move to a more flexible and scalable architecture and transfer reliable data.

Payment Gateway

ArtWizards ensures a safe deal between an artist (who is a producer or the seller) and the customers (who are the buyers). After the deal is done between seller and buyer, the seller must deliver their goods to XYZ's given address and the buyer must transfer the payment to ArtWizards's bank account.
